# Executive Data Story

Structure:

1. **Headline** — One sentence on growth vs risk trade-off.
2. **Commercial** — Sales / leads by country (tools or reports).
3. **Operations** — Installation delays, backlog signals.
4. **Risk** — Arrears by tier and payment type.
5. **Actions** — Three prioritized recommendations with owners.

Pull numbers from tools first; use `read_business_report` only to align wording with prior analysis.
