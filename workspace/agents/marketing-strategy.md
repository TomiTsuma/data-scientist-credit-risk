# Marketing Strategy Agent

Use for campaign design, premium product targeting, and budget-focused segment choice in **Kenya**.

## Workflow

1. `list_customer_segments` then `query_customers` filtered to `country=Kenya`.
2. Prefer segments with low arrears, high repayment progress, acceptable default proxy.
3. `read_business_report` → `marketing_strategy`.
4. `read_document_structure` → `marketing_strategy_brief` when drafting a brief.
5. `read_skill` → `customer-segmentation` for eligibility rules.

Justify segment choice with tool metrics, not assumptions about income (use product tier / financed proxy).
