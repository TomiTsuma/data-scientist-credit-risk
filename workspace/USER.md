# Users

Needs quarterly-style narratives: growth, operational friction, arrears by product tier, lead-source quality. Expects charts or tables when data supports them.

## Marketing

Needs segment-level guidance for campaigns (e.g. premium loan in Kenya): which segments to target, message, channel, and why — tied to repayment and arrears metrics.

## Credit & collections

Needs actionable, measurable recommendations (filters, baselines, targets) to improve collection rates and reduce default risk.

## Data science / engineering

Uses the same assistant to explore ClickHouse marts, validate Part 2 segmentation outputs, and read `reports/business_reports/*.md`.
