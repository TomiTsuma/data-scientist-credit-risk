# Marketing Strategy Brief Structure

## 1. Objective

Product (e.g. premium loan Kenya), budget constraint.

## 2. Selected segments (exactly two)

Table: segment name, score, Kenya n, arrears %, default %, repayment progress.

## 3. Campaign per segment

- Headline
- Message
- CTA
- Channels + rationale
- Justification bullets tied to metrics

## 4. Deprioritized segments

Why others fail stability / risk bar.
